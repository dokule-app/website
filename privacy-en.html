Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-10

Dokule takes the protection of your data seriously. This policy explains which data we collect, why we need it, and what rights you have.

1. Data Controller

The controller responsible for processing your personal data is Dokule. You can reach us at kontakt@dokule.com.

2. Data Collected

We collect the following data: email address, name, address, phone number, photos, GPS coordinates, timestamps, and vehicle/insurance details.

3. Purpose of Processing

The data is processed exclusively for tamper-proof damage documentation and the preservation of evidence.

4. Storage

All data is stored encrypted on servers located within the EU (AWS Frankfurt).

5. Sharing

Your data is only shared when you actively initiate it (e.g. when sending to your insurance). We never share data for advertising purposes.

6. QR Code

When exchanging a QR code with another party, only your name, insurance and license plate are shared — no other personal details.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify and erase your data at any time in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the app.

9. Cookies

Dokule does not use tracking cookies. Our website uses only technically necessary cookies.
